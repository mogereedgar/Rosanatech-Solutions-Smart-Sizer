Electrical FAQs

Electrical Installation FAQs

How long does electrical wiring take?

Most residential wiring projects take 3-7 days. New construction wiring may take 1-3 weeks depending on property size and complexity.

Do you provide NEMA/EPRA compliance certificates?

Yes! All our installations come with full compliance certificates and meet Kenya Bureau of Standards requirements.

Is the site survey fee refundable?

100% refundable! The site survey fee is deducted from your final installation cost if you proceed with our services.

What's your warranty period?

We offer:

  • 5-year workmanship warranty
  • 1-year free maintenance
  • Lifetime technical support
  • Compliance certificate guarantee
Do you handle permit applications?

Yes! We handle all NEMA, EPRA, and county government permit applications at no extra cost.

What safety standards do you follow?

We strictly follow IEEE standards, Kenya Bureau of Standards regulations, and international electrical safety codes.
