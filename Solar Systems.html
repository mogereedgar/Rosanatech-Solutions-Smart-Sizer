Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations take 2-5 days. Commercial projects may take 1-3 weeks depending on system size.

Do you provide maintenance?

Yes! We offer 1-year free maintenance and optional extended service plans.

Is the site survey fee refundable?

100% refundable! The site survey fee is deducted from your final installation cost if you proceed.

What's the warranty period?

We offer:

  • 25-year panel performance warranty
  • 10-year inverter warranty
  • 5-year workmanship warranty
Do I need EPRA approval?

For grid-tied systems above 500W, yes. We handle all EPRA paperwork for you at no extra cost.

Can I monitor my system?

Yes! All our systems come with mobile app monitoring so you can track production and savings in real-time.
