Maintenance FAQs

How often should I schedule maintenance?

Solar systems: Every 6 months. Electrical systems: Annually. Smart homes: Quarterly for optimal performance.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes! 24/7 emergency support with 2-4 hour response times for contract customers.

What's included in maintenance contracts?

Scheduled inspections, cleaning, software updates, priority service, and discounted repairs.

Can I get a one-time service?

Absolutely! We offer both subscription plans and one-time service visits.

Do you provide compliance certificates?

Yes, all maintenance includes EPRA/NEMA compliance checks and certification.

What's your response time for emergencies?

2-4 hours for Gold/Platinum members, same-day for all other emergency requests.
